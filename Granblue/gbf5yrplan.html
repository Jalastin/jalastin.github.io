Introduction to "The Five Year Plan"

This was when I got myself addicted into Granblue. I sat down and planned my progression out (dubbing it the Five Year Plan), and the first "year" of the plan was to get Europa's Tyros Zithers for my grid. 20 days of relentless grinding lead to 15 harps, which is more than the 12 drops I was aiming for.
